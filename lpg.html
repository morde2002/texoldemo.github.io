LPG

Texol LPG: Clean, Versatile, and Reliable Energy Solutions

Our Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) serves as a reliable and efficient energy source catering to diverse consumer needs. It's a clean and versatile fuel solution that powers homes, businesses, and industries.

Texol's commitment to quality, safety, and reliability makes its LPG offering a preferred choice for a wide range of customers seeking efficient and environmentally conscious energy solutions.

  • Versatile Energy Source: Texol LPG is a versatile fuel used for cooking, heating, hot water, and more, providing reliable energy across various applications.
  • Clean and Eco-Friendly: Known for its low carbon emissions, Texol LPG is an environmentally friendly alternative, reducing the carbon footprint compared to other fuels.
  • Diverse Cylinder Sizes: Offers various cylinder sizes suitable for different consumer needs, whether it's for household use or industrial applications.
  • Safety Measures: Emphasizing safety, Texol implements strict safety protocols and conducts regular checks to ensure secure handling and usage of LPG.
