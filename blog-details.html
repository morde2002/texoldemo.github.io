Texol Energies Company Official Launch

  • Nov 1, 2023
  • 12 Comments

Launch Speech by the CEO

Distinguished honorable minister, distinguished chairperson, National Planning Authority, commissioner, ministry of Energy and the invited guests, Thank you, I thank you all, thank you very much. Your presence on this very important day means a lot to all of the Texol team but, I personally take it with honor and humility. Today marks exactly 1 year since Texol Energies became operational, looking back at the anxiety that was present then, I owe much gratitude, and appreciation to my lord. I applaud my family, and my team, for their commitment, support, and dedication. Much appreciation to the management and staff of Texol Energy and Sta Café & Lounge for your continuous dedication towards the realization of our common dream. I take this opportunity to appreciate the support given by various stakeholders (the Ministry of Energy, Uganda Railway, UNOC, Mahati) for holding our hands as we strive to take our baby steps. Our clients, “You are the heroes of the moment.” You believed in us and stuck by us. We will walk this important journey together. Today, at this particular moment, I wish to announce our solid commitment to working closely with every stakeholder in this country. As we launch our company, I am proud to announce our mutual partnership with the boda boda fraternity.

As we unveil our fuel card system for individuals and co-operate, our pride in introducing the Texboda is unparalleled. We believe the contribution made by Boda Boda in this nation is great, hence this well-thought partnership. We urge all boda riders to obtain our cards free of charge in order to benefit from immense discounts, redeemable points, and even closer cooperation.

...With the help of the Almighty and the support of my fellow countrymen, we have walked this journey together...We have together transformed and set a solid platform that will make Uganda one of the largest Avocado export country in Africa.

I take this opportunity to announce a proposed partnership between Texol Energies, Musubi farm and the ministry of Environment. Beginning January 2024, we pledge to plant 100,000 trees, including fruit trees, in our schools and catchment areas. Furthermore, we have allocated funds for the construction of public toilets in designated locations around Namasuba.
Honorable Minister, Musubi farm will flag off Avocado oil export sometime in November, your presence will be highly appreciated. We are indeed grateful for the opportunity our sister company Kuku Chic is extending to thousands of out growers in the country to serve not only Uganda but the region. It’s our common good as a nation, to protect and preserve our fragile ecosystem. The government through the Ministry of Energy has embarked on a campaign to save our forests and water catchment areas. We are proud to be part of this ambitious plan. Texol Energies will launch its LPG plant in the course of 2024 In Sha Allah. It’s also my honor to inform you of our planned lubricant blending plant in collaboration with our partners Texol Lubritec Company from Dubai. The Directors of Texol Lubritec Company, Mr. Philip, Mr. Tausif, Mr. Rajiv. We are also planning to roll out in collaboration with Katwe Police Station a vigorous safety campaign of boda boda, through training and information on digital screens and print media. Honorable Minister, distinguished guests, it’s my pleasure to welcome you all to this occasion as we Redefine our Reliability.
It’s now my pleasure to invite the chairperson of the National Planning Authority.
