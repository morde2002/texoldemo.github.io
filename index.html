Welcome To Texol Energies Uganda

At Texol Energies Uganda, we are not just a company; we are a beacon of excellence in the oil and gas industry. Since our inception in 2022, we have been redefining the standards of oil and gas marketing with a strong presence boasting over 10 stations in and around Kampala.
