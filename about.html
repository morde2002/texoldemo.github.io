We are not just a company; we are a beacon of excellence in the oil and gas industry. Since our inception in 2022, we have been redefining the standards of oil and gas marketing with a strong presence boasting over 10 stations in and around Kampala. Our journey has been driven by a passionate and experienced management team, committed to delivering the highest quality products and services to our valued customers. Our Managing Director's unwavering dedication to excellent customer service has been the driving force behind our success.

Our Objectives

T - Technology Advancement:We invest in cutting-edge research and development to optimize our energy solutions and enhance the overall customer experience.
E - Environmental Stewardship: We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint, advocating for sustainable energy sources, and leading initiatives that prioritize the well-being of our planet.
X - Excellence in Service: Our goal is to consistently achieve outstanding service standards, ensuring that every interaction reflects our dedication to customer satisfaction.
O - Operational Efficiency: We continuously optimize our processes, infrastructure, and systems to ensure cost-effectiveness, speed, and reliability in delivering energy solutions.
L - Leadership in the Market: We position Texol Energies as an industry frontrunner through innovation, community outreach, and a strong brand presence.

Core Values


T - Transparency
Maintain open and honest communication with all stakeholders, fostering a culture of trust.

E - Empowerment
Encourage initiative and decision-making at all levels, nurturing growth and fostering a proactive workforce.

X - eXceed Expectations
Push boundaries and go beyond the norm, constantly seeking to surprise and delight our customers.

O - Ownership
Take responsibility for our actions, decisions and result, emphasizing accountability throughout the organization.

L - Loyalty
Cherish our long-standing relationships with customers, partners and employees, always prioritizing mutual respect and commitment.
